FEATURES

Everything a scanner should do — nothing it shouldn't.

Built lean on purpose. Every feature earns its place in a 12 MB app.

Auto edge detection

NepScan finds the four corners of your page the moment it enters frame — on textbooks, ID cards, receipts, or curled paper — and crops it clean automatically.

Nepali + English OCR

Extract selectable, searchable text in देवनागरी and English from the same page — copy it straight into Word or WhatsApp.

Batch scanning

Capture a whole assignment or file of receipts in one session — pages queue automatically into a single PDF.

One-tap enhance

Shadows, creases, and yellowed paper are cleaned up automatically so your scan looks freshly printed.

On-device processing

Scans are processed on your phone by default — nothing leaves your device unless you choose to export or share.

Export anywhere

PDF, JPG, or PNG — share straight to WhatsApp, email, or Google Drive without leaving the app.

HOW IT WORKS

From paper to PDF in four steps.

1

Open the camera

Launch NepScan and point it at any document — the viewfinder starts tracking edges in real time.

2

Capture & auto-crop

Tap the shutter, or let hands-free mode fire automatically once the page is steady and in frame.

3

Enhance & extract text

Pick a filter — B&W, color, or grayscale — and run OCR to pull out editable Nepali or English text.

4

Export & share

Save as PDF or image and send it wherever it needs to go — no watermark stamped on top.

ABOUT

Built by Kharayoo Innovation, for how Nepal actually works.

Kharayoo Innovation is a small software studio based in Kathmandu. We started NepScan after watching students and shopkeepers wrestle with scanner apps that were bloated, ad-heavy, and blind to Nepali text.

NepScan is our answer: a fast, focused scanning tool that reads Devanagari as well as it reads English, and respects that not everyone has an unlimited data plan or the latest phone.
